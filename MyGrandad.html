For my interesting family member, I chose my Grandfather Bob. Personally, I think my Grandfather is the most interesting person i’ve ever met. He has taught me to not take what I have for granted and how much I should appreciate my life. My grandad is also my hero because he served in WWII. When he was my age, he was working on a horse farm and going to school. When he was drafted, he made a promise to come back and carry on the bloodline and that’s exactly what he did. If it were not for my GrandFather Bob I wouldn’t have the same perspective on the world and life as I do now and that’s why I chose him as the most interesting person in my family.
